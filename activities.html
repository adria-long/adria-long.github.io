Our Activities

MAES hosts a mix of professional development and community-centered events throughout the semester. Whether you’re looking to grow your skills or find your people, there’s something for everyone.

General Meetings

Our regular meetings keep members connected through updates, guest speakers, and professional development topics. Come for the info, stay for the community.

Workshops

Hands-on sessions focused on building technical and career skills—resume reviews, interview prep, tools, and more. Practical, supportive, and beginner-friendly.

Study Hours

The weekly get together where we study with our fellow MAES members, have the chance to ask for academic help and have a fun time together.

Socials

Social events that bring members together outside of meetings, good vibes, bonding, and a welcoming space to connect across majors.

Community Service

Volunteer opportunities that let us give back and make an impact in the local community. Meaningful work + doing it together.

Familias

Our Familia program is where members are sorted into groups that are led by 2 upperclassman. They then form a Familia that do various activities together.

Banquet

The annual MAES banquet is where we look to the future while reminiscing on the past year together.
