About MAES

Who We Are

MAES is an inclusive organization that develops STEM leaders in academic, executive, and technical communities. Our Texas A&M chapter builds a welcoming environment where members grow professionally while staying connected to their culture and community. We learn from one another, build lasting friendships, and create familia.

Our Mission

To promote, cultivate, and advance representation of Latino leadership and education in STEM.

Our Vision

Be the foremost Latino organization for the development of STEM leaders in academic, executive, and technical communities. Create an everlasting Aggie familia that supports one another when it matters most.

Our Pillars
